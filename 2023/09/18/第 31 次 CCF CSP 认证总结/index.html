3. 梯度求解

考虑构造表达式树，然后对树根节点求导，通过递归可以得到最终答案，假设节点的值为 \(rt\)，则有：

  • 若 \(rt\) 表示常数，则 \(\frac{\partial rt}{\partial x_i} = 0\)
  • 若 \(rt\) 表示 \(x_j\)，则 \(\frac{\partial rt}{\partial x_i} = [x_j = x_i]\)
  • 若 \(rt\) 表示某个符号 \(\oplus\)，其左右节点的值分别是 \(u\) 和 \(v\) ：
    • 加法（\(rt = u + v\)）：\(\frac{\partial rt}{\partial x_i} = \frac{\partial u}{\partial x_i} + \frac{\partial v}{\partial x_i}\)
    • 减法（\(rt = u - v\)）：\(\frac{\partial rt}{\partial x_i} = \frac{\partial u}{\partial x_i} - \frac{\partial v}{\partial x_i}\)
    • 乘法（\(rt = uv\)）：\(\frac{\partial rt}{\partial x_i} = \frac{\partial u}{\partial x_i} v + \frac{\partial v}{\partial x_i}u\)
